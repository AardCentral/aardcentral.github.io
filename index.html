AardCentral

This site was created to serve as a centralized hub for all scripts related to Aardwolf. There are several players who contribute to a quality-of-life playing experience, but one generally has to know who the players are and what they have to offer. By keeping a central hub, players can bookmark this site and see what others have to offer.

If your site is missing, please submit an issue to have it added.

Disclaimer: All efforts are made to ensure the scripts are legal for use with Aardwolf. If someone finds a questionable script, please verify it is legal before using it. Report questionable scripts to Crowley so that they may be removed until investigated. Thank you. Additionally, if you find a questionable script and choose to use it anyway, all consequences are your own and I hold no responsibility for your actions. Please follow the rules of Aardwolf.
